Alien Invasion

In this online Space Invaders game, protect the earth from the Alien Invasion; use the Arrow Right/Left keys to move your spaceship, and use the Space Bar to shoot at the aliens. You can hide from the alien spaceships behind shields, and try to hit the flying saucer for bonus points.
